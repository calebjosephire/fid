Not is our food good, it's also good-looking! Our patrons often stop to admire our fare with a quick Instagram befire digging in. We've collected a few of our favorite shots here.

Our Baked Goods


We start our day at the crack of dawn to bake our own muffins, bread, and dinner rolls. Loaves not used that day are donated to the local food shelter.

Our Burgers


People come from all over to enjoy our lovely made burgers. We grind our own locally-soucred organic beef and turkey so you know it's fresh and free from fillers and other nonsense. Go for one of our creative topping combos or stick with the classics.

Catch of the Day


Our chef works with local fishermen to pick the freshest the sea has to offer for our daily seafood special. Our Roast Cod Caponata with Roasted Potatoes is an aol favorite with our regulars.

Our Chicken Tenders


One of Americas favorite dishes made fresh. Come enjoy chicken tenders, with freshly cooked chicken and the bread crumb surroundings. It's enjoyable for all people, mostly the picky eaters.

Crispy French Fries


The main side dish for all kinds if meals are freshly made crispy fries. It's enjoyable with our burgers and chicken tenders. Don't forget the sauses, ketchup, bbq, what ever you may like!

Fresh Tabouleh


Try our Tabouleh salad, it is made fesh with all kinds of vegetables in it from fresh gardens. It is perfect for appatizers before a big meal!
