Tampa Bay Buccaneers Transactions

A list of what has happened during the 2024 Offseason with Key signings and departures of the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.

Signings

  • Agreed to Terms with Liam Coen (Offensive Coordinator).
  • Agreed to Terms with Mike Evans to a 2-year deal (Wide Receiver).
  • Re-Signed Devon Thompkins to a 1-year deal (Wide Receiver).
  • Agreed to Terms with Baker Mayfield to a 3-year term (QuarterBack).
  • Re-Signed Greg Gains to a 1-year deal (Defensive Line).
  • Agreed to Terms with Lavonte David to a 1-year deal (Linebacker).
  • Agreed to Terms with Jordon Whitehead to a 2-year deal (Safety).
  • Re-Signed Chase Edmonds to a 1-year deal (Runningback).
  • Re-Signed Chase McLaughlin to a 3-year deal (Kicker).
  • Agreed to Terms with Randy Gregory to a 1-year deal (Outside Linebacker).
  • Re-Signed William Gholston to a 1-year deal (Defensive Line).

Departures

  • Released Shaquil Barrett, signed with Miami Dolphins (Outside Linebacker).
  • Traded Carlton Davis and a 2024 third-round pick to the Detriot Lions, for a 2024 sixth-round pick and 2025 sixth-round pick (Cornerback).
  • Free Agent Devin White signs with Philadelphia Eagles (Linebacker).
